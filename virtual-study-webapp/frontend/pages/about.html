ClouDy: Your study space on the cloud

ClouDy aims to enhance your learning experience by connecting you with your peers anytime, anywhere. Through interactive video calls, you can collaborate, share knowledge, and stay motivated while studying together in real time. Whether you are preparing for exams, working on projects, or seeking support, ClouDy provides a safe and engaging environment to help you achieve your academic goals.
