ClouDy aims to create a conducive environment for individuals to study together.

To ensure that the platform is conducive for everyone, users should adhere to the guidelines below:

  1. Treat all participants with kindness and respect. Harassment, bullying, or discriminatory remarks will not be tolerated.
  2. Use Appropriate Language. Inappropriate content or language will result in removal from the session.
  3. If you experience or witness any behaviour that violates these rules, please report it to the platform moderators immediately.

Thank you for being part of the ClouDy community. Happy studying!
